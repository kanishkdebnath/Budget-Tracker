Design foundations

Tokens that everything else inherits from. Material 3 Expressive, anchored to your brand navy #0d2736 as the primary identity. Income / expense use semantic green / red — the data signal, separate from the brand voice. A warm amber tertiary balances the cool palette for Plan banners and recurring-due highlights.

Color · brand palette

Navy is the app's voice — buttons, top bar, FAB, the active-nav pill. Semantic green / red are the signal — they only appear on amounts and deltas, never as surface. This separation keeps "is this money good or bad?" instantly readable while the chrome stays brand-cohesive.

Typography

Inter as the brand font with system fallback. M3 type scale, lightly tuned. Tabular numerals are mandatory anywhere money appears so digits align in columns. Headings use slight negative letter-spacing; labels are 12px upper-case for the calm "editorial" feel within a Material shell.
